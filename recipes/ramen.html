Japanese Ramen

What is Japanese Ramen?

Ramen is a popular Japanese dish featuring wheat noodles in a savory broth, often served with delicious toppings like sliced pork and soft-boiled eggs.

Quick Info

  • Prep time: 20 mins
  • Cook time: 30 mins
  • Total time: 50 mins
  • Yield: 4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 1 lb pork belly, sliced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp miso paste
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 lb fresh ramen noodles
  • 4 scallions, chopped
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs, halved
  • Nori sheets, sliced (optional)

How to Cook

  1. In a large pot, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add sliced pork belly and cook until browned and crisp. Transfer to a plate and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  3. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, miso paste, sugar, and sesame oil. Add the mixture to the pot and stir until combined.
  5. Add fresh ramen noodles to the pot and cook according to the package instructions.
  6. Once the noodles are cooked, remove from heat and add chopped scallions and baby spinach. Stir until the spinach is wilted.
  7. Divide the ramen and broth into bowls. Top each bowl with pork belly slices, soft-boiled eggs, and sliced nori sheets, if desired.

Enjoy your delicious bowl of homemade Japanese ramen soup!
