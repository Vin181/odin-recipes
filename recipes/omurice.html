Omurice

What is Omurice?

Omurice is a popular Japanese dish consisting of a fluffy omelette wrapped around savory fried rice.

Quick Info

  • Prep time: 10 mins
  • Cook time: 20 mins
  • Total time: 30 mins
  • Yield: 2 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 small carrot, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp sake or dry sherry
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 large eggs
  • Green onions, thinly sliced (optional)

How to Cook

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add chopped onion and carrot and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until slightly softened.
  2. Add minced garlic and stir-fry for an additional 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add cooked rice to the skillet and stir-fry with the vegetables until evenly combined.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, ketchup, sake or dry sherry, salt, and pepper. Pour the mixture over the rice and stir-fry until the rice is evenly coated.
  5. In a separate non-stick skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium heat. Whisk 2 eggs together in a small bowl and pour into the skillet. Swirl the skillet to distribute the eggs and cook until the edges are set and the center is still slightly runny.
  6. Place half of the fried rice on top of the omelette in the skillet. Fold the omelette over the rice and slide onto a plate.
  7. Repeat with the remaining 2 eggs and fried rice to make a second Omurice.
  8. Serve the Omurice hot, garnished with thinly sliced green onions if desired.

Enjoy your delicious and comforting Omurice, perfect for any time of day!
